Strategy Overview
Systematic Volatility Capture

Gamma Sigma is a crypto-native prop fund specialising in systematic options strategies across major liquid venues. The fund exploits persistent structural mispricings in implied volatility by maintaining a delta-neutral book with net positive convexity exposure.

Capital is deployed across two primary engines: a short-vega carry book that harvests the vol risk premium in rangebound conditions, and a long-gamma tail book that profits asymmetrically during dislocations. The combination targets positive expected value across all vol regimes.

Mechanics

Carry engine — Sells near-dated straddles on BTC/ETH when realised vol is trending below implied. Positions are sized by a vol-normalised Kelly criterion and delta-hedged intraday via perpetual swaps.

Tail engine — Maintains a rolling position in cheap convexity via ratio spreads and calendar structures. This leg is self-financing over a full cycle and provides the portfolio with positive skew exposure without meaningful carry drag.

Risk Factors
Known risks & mitigants
Risk Severity Description Mitigant
Vol Regime Shift Medium A sustained low-vol environment compresses carry premiums; a sudden vol spike can create short-term losses on the carry book before delta hedges adjust. Tail engine provides positive convexity offset. Stop-loss triggers at 8% monthly drawdown.
Venue Counterparty Medium Concentrated exposure to Deribit for options execution creates platform-specific settlement risk in an insolvency scenario. Max 60% AUM on any single venue. Active migration plan to Derive (on-chain) for >30% of options flow.
Liquidity Low Deep out-of-the-money options can widen significantly in spread during stress, increasing hedging costs. Portfolio maintains >25% in unencumbered stablecoins at all times. Redemptions gated to monthly.
Execution Slippage Low High-frequency delta hedging via perps introduces slippage in illiquid hours, eroding returns on the carry book margin. TWAP execution during off-peak hours. Internal slippage budget tracked per position and reported monthly.
