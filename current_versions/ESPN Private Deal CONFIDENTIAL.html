EGY Governance Token
EGY Encodes Protocol Growth

EGY is not a passive revenue token. It is a direct encoding of the protocol's growth trajectory. As ESPN TVL grows, it increases the leverage available in STRAT. As STRAT scales, it expands ESPN's capacity. EGY holders capture value across the entire flywheel: the more the protocol is adopted, the more EGY is worth.

STRAT Side · Management Fee

A fee on the ETH treasury AUM underwritten through STRAT. Scales with every new leveraged ETH seeker that bonds into the protocol.

Debt Side · Bonding Revenue

A share of ESPN bonding demand, income generated as capital flows into the vault. Grows as protocol adoption deepens on both sides of the stack.
